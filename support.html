Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a collectible?

Open the Scan tab, point your camera at the item's box barcode, and hold steady. The app will identify the SKU and add it to your collection. If the box is damaged or missing, switch to Sticker Text mode to recognize the SKU number, or Photo Match to find candidates by visual similarity.

Why are some items missing from the catalog?

The catalog covers vinyl figures, plush toys, and action figures across the major manufacturers, organized by in-universe era. Older or limited-release variants may not yet be indexed. If you find an item missing, please email us with the manufacturer, SKU, and any reference photos.

How are prices calculated?

Prices are aggregated from public marketplace data (sold listings on eBay) by our pricing backend and updated daily. Each item shows a low, mid, and high price band. Prices reflect the secondary market and are estimates, not guaranteed sale prices.

What's included in Fazbear Collector Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited collection size (free is capped at 100 items), the Variant Chase Radar (alerts when missing variants appear under your set price), historical price charts, advanced filters and smart lists, and priority catalog updates during new product waves.

How do I track variants of the same item?

Each base SKU in the catalog has a list of known variants (such as glow-in-the-dark, flocked, chase, blacklight, glitter, metallic). On the item detail screen, tap a variant chip to mark it as owned. You can own multiple copies of the same variant if you collect doubles.

Does the app sync across my devices?

Cloud sync is planned for a release after v1.0. The first version stores your collection locally on each device using Apple's SwiftData framework. You can export your collection at any time from the Settings screen.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Auto-renewable subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select Fazbear Collector to manage or cancel. The lifetime purchase does not auto-renew and does not need to be canceled.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your cloud-synced data within 30 days. Local data on your device is also cleared. If you only used the app locally and never enabled cloud sync, deleting the app from your device removes all data immediately.

The barcode scanner isn't working

Make sure the barcode is well-lit and clearly visible. Hold your phone steady about 6-8 inches from the box. If a box has a damaged or missing barcode, switch to Sticker Text mode to recognize the SKU number printed on the front.
